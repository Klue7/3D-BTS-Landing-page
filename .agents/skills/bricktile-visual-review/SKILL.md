name: bricktile-visual-review
description: Use when reviewing Brick Tile Shop pages for visual accuracy against local reference screenshots and product/install assets.
---

Review visually, not generically.

Steps:
1. Inspect /references and local product/install images first.
2. Compare current implementation to asset truth.
3. Check:
   - BTS branding
   - hero composition
   - section rhythm
   - product/install image matching
   - CTA prominence
   - dark premium feel
4. Return:
   - must-fix issues
   - should-fix issues
   - exact files/components likely responsible
5. Do not invent product facts or suggest unrelated stock imagery.
