---
name: bricktile-product-copy
description: Use when writing or editing Brick Tile Shop headings, CTAs, trust text, showcase copy, spec labels, or related-product text.
---

Write copy that is:
- concise
- factual
- premium
- architectural
- conversion-aware

Avoid:
- sports language
- filler marketing phrases
- invented specs
- claims unsupported by the product data or references
